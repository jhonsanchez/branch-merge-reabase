## Directions
(@) In large bowl sift:
- Flour
- Baking Powder
- Salt
- Sugar
(@) Combine milk, eggs and melted butter
- Mix until smooth
(@) Oil and heat griddle or frying pan
(@) Pour ~1/4 cup batter on griddle
(@) Brown on both sides and serve
