## Directions
(@) Mix
- Beef
- Onion
- Worcestershire
(@) Shape meat in 4 patties 3/4in thick
(@) Refrigerate 20 minutes
(@) Preheat Grill
(@) Season outside of burgers with Salt and Pepper
(@) Cook 5 minutes per side
(@) Let cool 5 minutes
