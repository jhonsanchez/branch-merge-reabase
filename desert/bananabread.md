## Directions
(@) Preheat oven to **350F**
(@) Mash
- Ripe bananas
(@) Stir into bananas
- Melted butter
(@) Mix
- Baking soda
- Salt
- Sugar
- Beaten egg
- Vanilla extract
(@) Mix
- Flour
(@) Pour batter into loaf pan
(@) Bake **50-60min**
Or until toothpick comes out clean
(@) Remove from oven and let cool in pan (**3-5min**)
