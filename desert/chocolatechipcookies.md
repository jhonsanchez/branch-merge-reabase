## Directions
(@) Preheat oven to **350F**
(@) Cream
- Butter
- White Sugar
- Brown Sugar
Until smooth
(@) Beat in
- eggs
- vanilla
(@) Add
- Baking Soda
- Water
(@) Add
- Flour
- Cinnamon
Recommended to do it 1 cup at a time
(@) Add
- Chocolate Chips
(@) Place 1 1/2 in balls of batter onto trays
(@) Bake for 10 minutes
Or until edges are lightly browned
